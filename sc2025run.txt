REM SHOW UDF WORKS BY MODIFY EXP BYTE OF TWO ARRAY ELEM

SCR
DIM A(2)
A(1)=16
A(2)=256

REM ADDRESSES OF EXP BYTES OF THE TWO ELEM OF ARRAY

A1=(5*8+7)*256+3
A2=(5*8+7)*256+7

 5.0

 4.0

 9.0

 1.0
